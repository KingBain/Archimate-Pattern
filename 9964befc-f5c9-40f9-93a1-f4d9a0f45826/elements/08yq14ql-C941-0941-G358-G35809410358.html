ISTG-33 Family Control Name INFORMATION SYSTEM RECOVERY AND RECONSTITUTION
ISTG-33 Supplemental Guidance Protection of backup and restoration hardware, firmware, and software components includes both physical and technical safeguards. Backup and restoration software includes, for example, router tables, compilers, and other security-relevant system software. Related controls: AC-3, AC-6, PE-3.
